Fraudulent Transaction Detection using ML

Business Problem: Millions of people fall victim to credit card fraud. Percentage of fraudulent transactions is only a small percentage of all transactions. Hence, a solution is expected to accurately identify any fradulent transactions and also at the same time not affecting genuine transactions. This project is an attempt to develop a model (XGBoost, LightGBM) with best recall measure and Concordance index.

Product Review/Feedback Analysis

This project consists of an end to end solution to Business for analysing the feedback received for products sold over E-commerce platform. The product was Jewelery category, due to unavailability of training data, it is extracted from Amazon platform, it was 14GB of JSON data. This project involves Web Scraper to retrieve product reviews from the E-commerce platform, Logistic regression model was developed as basic classifier and then advanced model using Keras is implemented. Dashboard using plotly and dash is prepared for the client to view the analysis results and test it with instant feedback for user input.

People Analytics

This project repository consists of 3 projects namely 1. Employee Churn detection 2. Employee Job Satisfaction Analysis 3. Evaluation of Training and Development Effectiveness. Employee Churn Detection: Employee turnover is the process of employees leaving the company, also known as employee churn or attrition, may result in high cost for the company. It may affect company's hiring or retention decisions Employee Job Satisfaction:Dissatisfaction leads to employee turnover, lower productivity, stress and conflict in workplace. Therefore, it is important to develop and ensure the job satisfaction of employees to benefit both the individuals and the organization. The analysis is carried out based on unsupervised algorithm association rule mining. Training and Development- Effectiveness Analysis: Technical skills enhancement is a major compononet to adhere to project roadmap. Measuring effectiveness of training delivered is one of the ROI metric. Here compare means techniques are used (t-Test, Anova test,paired t-tests).
